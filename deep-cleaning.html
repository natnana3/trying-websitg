Deep cleaning for a true reset.

We go beyond maintenance dusting—think built-up grease, soap film, door frames, and the corners that rarely see a cloth.

Great times to book a deep clean

  • First visit before starting a recurring plan
  • Spring cleaning or post-holiday recovery
  • After small remodels or painting touch-ups
  • Allergy season when you want vents and ledges detailed

Add-ons people often pair with deep cleans

Inside oven and refrigerator, interior windows and tracks, cabinet fronts in kitchen and bath, scrubbing grout lines, and wet-wiping baseboards through the home. Tell us your priorities—we’ll sequence the toughest jobs first.

How long it takes

Deep work scales with square footage, pets, and how long it’s been since a thorough pass. Share photos or a quick video in your message and we’ll estimate crew size and hours more accurately.
