Recurring plans

Same team when possible, predictable pricing, and priority rebooks.

Weekly visits keep high-traffic kitchens and baths consistently fresh. Biweekly works well for smaller households that still want a reliable reset. Monthly is ideal as a boost between your own light tidying.

We’ll suggest a rhythm after a walkthrough or a detailed description of your layout. Plans can include add-ons like inside the fridge, interior windows, or extra focus on pets and allergies.
