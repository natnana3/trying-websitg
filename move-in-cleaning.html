Move-in and move-out cleaning.

Landlords, sellers, and buyers in Frederick and nearby—we clean empty spaces top to bottom so handoffs are smooth and security deposits fairer.

Move-out focus

Inside cabinets and closets, appliance interiors (oven, fridge, microwave), scuff marks on walls where safe for paint, bathroom lime and soap buildup, garage sweep-out if requested, and final floor care so walkthrough photos look their best.

Move-in peace of mind

Even “clean” units collect dust after sitting empty. We sanitize kitchens and baths, wipe shelves before you unpack, and freshen flooring so your first night feels like yours—not the last tenant’s.

Coordination tips

  • Book after movers are completely finished.
  • Confirm utilities are on if we need hot water or power.
  • Leave parking or loading notes for equipment.
