Green cleaning options.

We can prioritize EPA Safer Choice–style products, unscented formulas, and microfiber systems that cut chemical use while still disinfecting where it matters.

Why clients ask for green cleaning

Young children crawling on floors, pets with sensitive paws, migraine triggers, or office policies that discourage heavy perfumes—all are good reasons to adjust the cart we bring.

What still gets sanitized

Food-prep surfaces and restrooms still meet hygienic standards; we match the right product to the surface (stone vs. quartz vs. laminate) so we clean effectively without unnecessary harshness.

How to request it

Mention “green preferred” in your estimate form. If you already buy brands you trust, we can use your supplies and adjust our checklist accordingly.
