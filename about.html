About Harp City Cleaning.

We started with a simple idea: show up on time, bring the right tools, and treat every key like it matters—because it does.

Who we are

Harp City Cleaning is a Frederick-based team serving homes and workplaces in Frederick, Maryland and nearby towns. We’re insured, background-checked, and trained on a written checklist so quality doesn’t depend on “who you got that day.”

How we hire and train

New cleaners shadow experienced leads before solo visits. We cover safe chemical handling, delicate surfaces, and customer communication—like texting when we’re on the way or if we’re running behind.

Our promise

Fair quotes, transparent add-ons, and a redo policy when we miss something we agreed to clean. Browse services or read the FAQ, then reach out for a custom plan.
