Residential cleaning that fits your routine.

Apartments, townhomes, and houses—we dust, vacuum, mop, sanitize baths, and reset kitchens so you come home to calm.

What a standard visit usually covers

Every Frederick-area home is different, but most maintenance cleans include dusting reachable surfaces, emptying trash, vacuuming carpets and rugs, hard-floor mopping, wiping counters and stovetops, cleaning sinks and fixtures, scrubbing toilets, showers/tubs wiped down, and mirrors polished.

When to add a deep clean

If it’s your first booking, after a renovation, or you haven’t had pros in a while, start with a deep clean so maintenance visits stay efficient. See our deep cleaning page for extras like baseboards, interior cabinets, and inside the oven.

Before we arrive

  • Pick up clutter so we can reach floors and surfaces.
  • Note fragile items or rooms to skip in your booking message.
  • Secure pets—we love them, but safety comes first.
  • Leave a spare key or entry instructions if you won’t be home.
