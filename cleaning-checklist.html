Room-by-room cleaning checklist.

Use this as a guide for maintenance visits. Deep cleans and move-outs often add everything marked “optional” below.

Kitchen

  • Countertops and backsplash
  • Stovetop and range front
  • Sink, faucet, and drain rim
  • Exterior of appliances
  • Inside microwave (standard)
  • Trash out, fresh liner

Optional: oven interior, fridge interior, cabinets inside.

Bathrooms

  • Toilet bowl, seat, base
  • Shower/tub walls and door
  • Sink, vanity, mirror
  • Tile spot-clean and fixtures
  • Trash out

Optional: grout scrub, exhaust fan cover.

Living & bedrooms

  • Dust surfaces, shelves, décor
  • Vacuum carpets and rugs
  • Hard floors damp-mopped
  • Empty trash bins

Optional: baseboards, blinds, ceiling fans.

Whole home / add-ons

  • Interior windows (reachable)
  • Doors and light switches
  • Stair treads and railings
  • Entryway and mudroom

Optional: garage sweep, laundry fold.

Lists vary by quote—tell us what matters most when you request an estimate. Compare with our deep cleaning page for heavy-duty tasks.
