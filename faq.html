Frequently asked questions.

Straight answers about how we work, what to expect, and how to get the best results.

  • Do I need to be home?

    Not unless you want to. Many clients provide a lockbox code or building access. We document entry instructions on your profile so the team is consistent.

  • Do you bring supplies?

    Yes—vacuums, mops, microfiber, and professional cleaners. If you prefer green or unscented products, say so when you book.

  • How do you price jobs?

    Mostly by square footage, bathrooms, and condition (pets, clutter, last professional clean). We’ll confirm a range before the first visit and adjust after the first walkthrough if needed.

  • What about pets?

    Friendly pets are welcome; aggressive or anxious animals should be secured so our cleaners can move safely with equipment.

  • Should I tip?

    Tips are never required. If you loved the visit, cash or app tips left for the crew are appreciated and split on multi-person jobs unless you specify otherwise.

  • What if something is missed?

    Contact us within 24 hours with a photo if possible. We’ll return to touch up the area at no charge when the miss is on us.
